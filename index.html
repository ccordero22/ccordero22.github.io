My Love

Carlos Cordero, 2019

My angel,
My sweetie,
My goddess,
My queen.

I took you for granted,
I didn't see your worth.
I played games with your mind,
And i toyed with your heart.

I put up a front,
I told myself we were just a fling.
I told myself we didn't have a future.
I made this my truth,
I had to protect my heart,
I had to keep my distance.

You come from a traditional Indian Muslim family.
I come from humble Christian roots.
You grew up with both parents.
I grew up in a broken home.

Your father raised you with a firm hand.
My father abandon me as an infant.
You grew up priviledged.
I grew up in poverty.

Despite all our differences,
Despite all our struggles,
We stand together,
Side by side.

I love you more than i believed possible.
I trust in you absolutely.
You are my home.
You are my future.
You are my inspiration.
You are my heart.
